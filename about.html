About the Creators

My background is multi-faceted, I have a degree in French literature, and experience in human resources and recruiting. Programming is an extension of my passion for learning human languages. Andrew Smith

Software Developer

Before becoming a software developer, I served in the Air Force for six years. I met my wife almost a year before I separated. She happened to be my flight attendant on a work trip I was on. I spent the last year (and even a few months after) of the military contemplating what to do upon my military separation date. I became focused on becoming a firefighter somewhere on the East Coast. Fortunately that did not work out. My wife (fiance at the time) pitched the idea of moving from the East Coast to Seattle. That idea became a reality very quickly. After a 45 hour car ride, we found ourselves in the beautiful, pacific northwest. My wife threw around the thought of turning technology into a career. I was hooked after writing my first line of code. I attended Code Fellows Code 102 course in August of 2019. I decided not continue with Code Fellows but to pursue the traditional college route. After two quarters at community college, I quickly realized I made a mistake. I retook code 102 in April of 2020, only this time my wife was accompanying (and surpassing me).
